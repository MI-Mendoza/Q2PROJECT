Image Description
Mount Apo, also known locally as Apo Sandawa, is a massive dormant solfataric stratovolcano on the Philippine island of Mindanao. It is the highest mountain in the Philippine Archipelago, Mindanao, and the 24th-highest peak of an island on Earth, rising 2,954 meters above sea level. .
Mount Pinatubo is an active stratovolcano in the Zambales Mountains, straddling the Philippine provinces of Zambales, Tarlac, and Pampanga in Central Luzon on the northern island of Luzon. Most people were unaware of its eruptive history until to the pre-eruption volcanic activity in early 1991.
Mayon, also known as Mount Mayon and Mayon Volcano, is an active stratovolcano in the Philippine province of Albay. It is a popular tourist destination known for its "perfect cone" due to its symmetric conical shape and is considered sacred in Philippine folklore. .
